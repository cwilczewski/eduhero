During this time our daughter Frances went from struggling and not confident in math to more confident and more self-reliant to the point of not needing a tutor in grade 11. Her grades immediately improved from C’s to B’s. Math is not her thing and he helped her get through it.

John is fully comfortable with math and teaching it to others, and is able to make a stressed-out student gain confidence and do better. Words Frances and I use to describe John’s math tutoring include patient, kind, thorough, available (let her know she could reach out any time) and caring. He took the time to explain, and he created practice work tailored to our daughter’s needs. He always checked to confirm understanding, and did not move on without ensuring understanding.

He is a natural and patient teacher and we asked his employer for him by name in the years after our first year with him. We have no hesitation in recommending John as a tutor to any math student.
