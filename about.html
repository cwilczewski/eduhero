About EduHero

Some more information about this service

EduHero was started by me, John Silva. With years of tutoring and teaching experience, particularly in the fields of Mathematics and Science, I can help students who need some extra help get back on track with their studies. This includes a number of services such as private in-home tutoring and editing of lab reports. EduHero can also provide essay writing help and editing. Currently the EduHero team consists of its founder, but is looking to expand in the near future.

With an honours Bachelor of Science (hB.Sc) degree and a Master of Teaching (MT) degree, I am fully equipped with knowledge of content in Science and Mathematics. Furthermore, my background has provided me with expertise in the school curriculum, and setting students up for success by also helping them to develop study habits that allows them to achieve their goals. Last but not least, I am an Ontario Certified Teacher (OCT).
