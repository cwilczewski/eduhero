Services

Whether your child is in elementary school or late into their high school career, EduHero can help you address your child’s areas of need. Below are some of the current subject offerings or services, which EduHero will expand in the near future.

Math

Helping your child with understanding problem-solving, what to look for in questions, test preparation, crash courses for next year’s class, and reviewing past areas of need to strengthen current skills.

Science

Science courses can have some difficult concepts to grasp, and writing lab reports can sometimes feel a little daunting. EduHero can provide much needed support and help your child.

English

Writing essays becomes a large part of several courses in high school, and beyond. Knowing what makes a good essay and structuring arguments is imperative to successful essay writing – and writing in general.
